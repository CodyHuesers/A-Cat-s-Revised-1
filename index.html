Meet The Artist

Hi, my name is Amy Sylvester! I'm a free-lance artist based in Lafayette, LA. I work in multimedia art including: acrylic, watercolor, gouache, and digital media; but I'm always learning and trying new mediums! I love connecting with new people, hearing their stories, and bringing their visions to life through my work.
